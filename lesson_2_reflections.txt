
1) What happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?

The metadata directory .git is created.  Without initialization there can
be no version control.

2) How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?

Staging area is a temporary storage area for files you would like to
commit.  The staging area allows you to commit one logical change at a
time.
